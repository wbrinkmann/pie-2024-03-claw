What is Clobstah Law?

This project was originally inspired by an event at Olin College of Engineering called Ratober where everyone who wishes to learn how to sew can come to learning events of sewing rats in October. This produces a lot of plush animals which need to be housed. Combine this with a team of five engineering students who have 6 weeks to complete a final project and the end result is a claw machine for rat plushies.
