Our Goal

We had one main objective: make a functioning claw machine that could pick up a plushie, hold it, move it, and drop it at a designated location. We wanted to develop this with a very small budget, design for functionality and robustness, and allow for an easy user interface.

Meet the Team

The team consists of five current sophomores who all worked together to produce the final machine. Each of us had primary areas we focused on, however a lot of cross collaboration occurred, including for the electrical system.
